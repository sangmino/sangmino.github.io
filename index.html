Sangmin Simon Oh [oh@gsb.columbia.edu]

Assistant Professor of Finance, Columbia Business School


My research is broadly within economics and finance, focusing on asset pricing, investments, and insurance. My work focuses on how institutional frictions impact the pricing of risk, with a focus on market dynamics and investor behavior and often leveraging machine learning tools.


I obtained my Ph.D. from the Joint Program in Financial Economics at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business and the Kenneth C. Griffin Department of Economics. Before graduate school, I graduated from the Jerome Fisher Program in Management & Technology (M&T) at the University of Pennsylvania with a B.S. in Finance and an M.S./B.S. in Electrical Engineering. Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked at AQR Capital Management and Forefront Capital Management.
