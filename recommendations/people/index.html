People

People who are hard to substitute.

Podcasters

Ben Gilbert & David Rosenthal

Acquired Podcast

Deep-dive podcast on company histories and business strategy. Episodes are meticulously researched, often running 3+ hours.

Writers

Michael Lewis

Author

Narrative nonfiction writer known for Liar's Poker, Moneyball, The Big Short, and Flash Boys. Unmatched ability to make complex financial topics accessible.

Paul Graham

Essayist, Y Combinator Co-founder

Essays on startups, programming, and ideas. His writing on doing what you love, wealth creation, and startup strategy shaped a generation of founders.

Matt Levine

Bloomberg Opinion Columnist

Daily newsletter on finance, law, and corporate absurdity. Former Goldman banker and lawyer who makes securities regulation genuinely entertaining.

Kevin Kelly

Founding Editor of Wired

Technologist and writer exploring the intersection of technology, culture, and human flourishing. Known for "1,000 True Fans" and annual birthday wisdom lists.

Tyler Cowen

Economist, George Mason University

Prolific blogger and polymath. Marginal Revolution covers economics, culture, food, and ideas with relentless curiosity. Also hosts Conversations with Tyler.
