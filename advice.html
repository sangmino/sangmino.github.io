  7. Andrew Huberman's 7-steps for a long healthy life:
    1. Get sunlight in your eyes first thing in the morning every day. 20-30 minutes is ideal
    2. Delay caffeine consumption for 90-120 minutes after waking.
    3. Every 24 hours, we need to re-up exercise. Weight-training for 3 days. Cardio for 3 days, where 80% of cardio in zone 2 (be able to talk). Sauna and cold cycle in 1 day.

    6. Take a hot shower / bath in the evening.
    7. For sleep, cool dark room and same bedtime every day.
